Case Study · E-Commerce · Shopify
Younique Creations Multi-Brand Shopify Store
E-CommerceShopifyMulti-Brand
View Live Site →
The Business

Younique Creations is an umbrella brand covering multiple product lines: jewellery under their main label, birthday decor through Decofy, baking accessories through TrendyTrolley, and bridal and baby shower collections. Four distinct product categories, each with its own identity, all needing to live under one roof.

The Challenge

The challenge was structural: how do you build a single Shopify store that houses multiple product lines with distinct identities without confusing customers, without fragmenting the brand, and without making it impossible for different types of shoppers to find what they came for? A generic multi-category store wouldn't work. The architecture needed to be intentional.

What Was Built

A structured Shopify store with clean navigation architecture that separates product categories and brand identities without fragmenting the shopping experience. Each sub-brand has its own collection space while sharing a unified checkout. Product pages were built for mobile-first browsing the primary channel for their target audience with social links integrated to drive traffic from Instagram directly into purchase flow. The store makes it easy for a customer looking for birthday decor to find it immediately, without being confused by jewellery options, and vice versa.

The Business

Essence Elevators designs, manufactures, and installs elevators for residential buildings, commercial properties, and housing developers across India. A high-consideration purchase where trust and perceived quality are the primary decision factors.

The Challenge

In elevator procurement, businesses and developers don't buy without confidence in the company behind the product. Essence Elevators needed a website that reflected the premium, precision-engineered nature of their products and could hold its own when being evaluated alongside established competitors during a vendor shortlisting process. The website was being judged before the sales conversation even started.

What Was Built

A premium WordPress website with sophisticated scroll-triggered animations, a full product and elevator type showcase covering every category they manufacture, and detailed About and contact sections designed to pass the credibility check during vendor evaluation. The visual language refined, minimal, and confident directly mirrors the quality of the physical product. Built with the Hellix theme architecture for smooth, consistent performance across devices. The design says premium before the visitor has read a single word.
