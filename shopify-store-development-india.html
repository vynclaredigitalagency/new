FAQ

Shopify Questions,
Answered Directly.

Can I use Razorpay with a Shopify store in India?+

Yes. Razorpay integrates directly with Shopify and is the most widely used payment gateway for Indian Shopify stores. Vynclare handles the full Razorpay setup as part of every Shopify build — including UPI, net banking, cards, and EMI options. PayU and Stripe are also supported if your business requires them.

How much does Shopify store development cost in India?+

Shopify store development costs depend on the number of products, custom design requirements, and integrations needed. Every store is built from scratch without pre-purchased themes. Contact us for a quote specific to your product catalogue and business requirements — we respond within 24 hours.

How long does it take to set up a Shopify store?+

A Shopify store with full product catalogue setup, payment gateway integration, and mobile-optimised product pages typically takes 3–5 weeks from briefing to launch. The timeline depends on catalogue size and the number of custom features required. All timelines are confirmed at the project start.

Do you set up Instagram shopping integration?+

Yes. Vynclare sets up Instagram and Facebook shopping integration as part of every Shopify build. This connects your product catalogue directly to your Instagram posts and stories, enabling customers to purchase without leaving the app — a significant driver of D2C sales for Indian brands.
