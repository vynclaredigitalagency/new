FAQ

Common Questions

How much does a website cost in India?+

Vynclare website costs vary by scope. A standard business website starts from a competitive price point for Indian businesses. E-commerce stores are priced based on catalogue size and feature requirements. Premium corporate builds with animations and multi-page architecture are quoted on scope. Contact us for a tailored quote — we respond within 24 hours.

What is the difference between a WordPress and a Shopify website?+

WordPress is best for service businesses, corporates, NGOs, and content-heavy websites — it offers maximum flexibility and is ideal for SEO-focused builds. Shopify is built specifically for e-commerce — it handles inventory, payments, and checkout flows out of the box. We recommend the right platform based on your business model during our discovery call.

Do you build websites for businesses outside India?+

Yes. Vynclare has built websites for businesses in the UAE, the United States, and India. The full project process — briefing, design, revisions, and delivery — runs remotely. We are experienced working across time zones and international market contexts.

Can you redesign my existing website?+

Yes. We audit your existing site, identify what's working and what isn't, and build the new version from scratch — not as a theme modification. The result is a faster, better-structured, more conversion-focused website that looks nothing like your previous one.

Do you offer website maintenance after launch?+

Yes. Every project includes 30 days of post-launch support at no extra cost. After that, monthly maintenance plans are available covering security monitoring, plugin updates, speed optimisation, content changes, and ongoing on-page SEO improvements.
